
# Scoring System

Every kebab is judged across 4 core criteria.

## 1. Aesthetic (1–5)
Presentation only.
Wrap structure, meat layering, sauce distribution, chip colour.

## 2. Quality (1–5)
Taste, freshness, seasoning, texture balance.

## 3. Price
£ = Cheap  
££ = Standard  
£££ = Premium  

## 4. Value
- Amazing Deal
- Good
- Okay
- Not worth

## Overall Score
Weighted combination of aesthetic, quality and perceived value.
